## Passport data

⚠️ Be careful when choosing where to register SIM cards. The less prestigious the location, the more likely it is to leak passport scans, which are required when registering a number. Choose official carrier stores; locations where the maximum range of services is provided for both individuals and legal entities.

## Known major leaks

In February 2022, there was a [leak of the Yandex.Food delivery database](https://habr.com/ru/news/t/654039/).
The names and surnames of clients, phone numbers,
full delivery addresses and order comments were published.
In total, almost 7 million unique numbers from Russia, Kazakhstan, and Belarus, with dates from June 19, 2021 to February 4, 2022.

To check their data, enthusiasts set up the service https://saverudata.info/.

## How do I remove myself from leaks?

**You can't.** You can ask popular services to [remove your record](./deleteme.md#removing-information-about-leaks),
but you won't do anything about the huge number of database copies that have already spread into many hands, private and closed services.
